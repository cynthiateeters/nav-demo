Hello! I am a totally fictional character created to demonstrate front-end web design and development. While I attended my wonderful community college, I took a web page development class because I was interested in creating my own website. I have always been a hands-on type of learner and I loved the challenge of doing something I had never done before. The experience was life-changing. I quickly realized how much I enjoyed working with computers and doing web development.

So, it was off to Web Page Development II course where we learned advanced HTML and CSS. We also got an introduction to JavaScript and JQuery. I can't tell you the satisfaction I receive from being able to make something of my own creation and seeing it right in front of me. The icing on the cake for me is when I am able to take one of my creations and share it with the world.
